Our Trucks

Established in 2016, Rober Haulings specializes in crafting custom-built, high-performance trucks tailored to meet the needs of our clients. With a commitment to quality, innovation, and durability, we pride ourselves on delivering trucks that combine power, reliability, and superior craftsmanship for both commercial and personal use.

Our Trucks

The Mack® Granite® is a heavy-duty truck built for the toughest jobs, featuring a fuel-efficient MP®8 engine with up to 505 horsepower and 1,860 lb.-ft. of torque. It delivers an average mileage of 5-7 MPG, depending on load and terrain. The mDRIVE™ HD transmission ensures optimal performance and fuel efficiency for construction, waste, and vocational tasks.
